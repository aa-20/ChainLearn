About The Developer

Aaron Aina

Aaron Aina is a 21 year old computer science student based in London,England who is currently studying at Newcastle University. He got the inspiration for ChainLearn by combining two of his passions namely blockchain technology and web development together to form an online learning platform for his final year project.

Ultimately, he strongly believes that blockchain will be a vessel for bringing about a better future, therefore the goal of ChainLearn is to educate society on the endless capabilities that blockchain technology can bring forth. ChainLearn was intentionally made free so that everybody across different backgrounds can benefit from the best online resources for blockchain. ChainLearn is open sourced, so that people with a high level of expertise in the field of blockchain can freely contribute their knowledge to improve not only the learning experience for the users, but the overall quality of the website.
